CAS CS132

Geometric Algorithms

Boston University

Fall 2023

This course is an introduction to linear algebra with an bent towards applications in computer science. One of its primary goals is to prepare students for courses in the CS cirriculum at BU which require some degree of linear algebra. It is based on (and depends heavily on the material of) the version of the course taught by Mark Crovella.
