This is my poetry.

loss

now you are
gone
and the ribbon
you fluttered
through spacetime
will fray

and I pray
that some strand
accounts for
your being

that you may
revisit your
favorite folds

(for grandma)

time

idle more
if you can

melt your
glass shard
thoughts
and make fear
not gone
but empty

until the hums
and sizzles
of your gut
heart
toes
etcetera

wake you to their
vague organic
doings

winter

walking
opposite me
through the
quadrangle
starring at
the tip-tops
of trees
leafless as
they are in
december

with a gentle
expression
not a smile
better maybe
but what
does it matter

the sun
showed pink
in her
cheeks

spring

rabbits in
the yard
again
not the same
as last year
i'd imagine

though
i suppose
i have never known
the movements
and patterns
of those creatures
